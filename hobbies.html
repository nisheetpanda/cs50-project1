My Hobbies

Over the many years, I have passed time through a variety of activities. Ever since I was little, I've loved LEGOs. While I got less time to play with them as I got older, I eventually discovered a tool called LEGO Digital Designer.

A couple years later, I watched a video on how the LEGO movie was made. In addition to LDD, they used a program called Maya. Because Maya was expensive, I didn't try using it. I started to take Khan Academy's Pixar in the Box courses, and one day a lecturer mentioned the program Blender, which I installed and started to learn. After a while, I started to learn Blender and make short movies like the Lego movie.

Another one of my favorite things to do is code. My favorite things to code are algorithmic problems. My favorite language is C++, due to it's power and speed. I tend to use Visual Studio Code and Vim for coding. Another computer science contest I really enjoy are CTFs. I have tried a couple different categories in it, and my favorite is forensics.

Another thing I do to destress is play videogames. I primarily play open world and action adventure games. My two favorite games are A Link to the Past and Minecraft, the former for it's entertaining story and gameplay, and the latter for its freedom.

I also enjoy watching TV shows and movies. My favorite TV shows are mystery but I also like comedy. My favotite movies are usually scifi or animated. My favorite movie is The Social Network. I also read books, and my favorite genres are fantasy, scii, nonfiction, and realsitic fiction.

I also really enjoy sports analytics. I first started exploring the subject last year, when I was trying to vote on what players should be on the MLB all star team.s
